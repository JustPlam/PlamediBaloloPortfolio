UX/UI Design

Google Keep (Notes App)

Problem:

Google Keep is useful, but the layout can feel a little messy, and it can be hard to organize notes quickly.


Design Idea:

I created a cleaner design that makes folders the main focus. Each folder (Priority, Work, Ideas, Personal) has its own color, making it easy to spot and organize notes. A simple search bar and a quick-add button help users find and create notes faster.


Outcome:

The new design is more organized, colorful, and user-friendly. It helps users save time, stay focused, and keep their notes neat in one place.
